Odin Recipe: Devin's New Hot Pot Recipe

This is by no means a real hot-pot recipe to preface. The joy of cooking comes out through experimentation of ingredients and flavors we like. This is one personal recipe that I came up with over trying to learn new coding skillz. Hopefully you will enjoy this as well!

What ingredients are needed?

Ingredients are simple, this recipe is more of a budget one; feel free to add extra ingredients where you see fit. The taste I was going for centered around sweet and sour so just be mindful of what can compliment the fruity taste of teh sangria with the sour soy sauce

  • Crab meat
  • Shrimp
  • Broccoli
  • Potatoes
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sangria wine
  • Spice of choice

Cooking steps

    1) Place all ingredients into a ziplock bag to thaw for about 1-2 hours
    2) During thawing process, pour soy auce and vinegar into the bag along with a base of water to thaw out
    3) After waiting time, turn stove on (medium to medium high settings)
    4) Pour ziplock bag into pot with ingredients and boil for 30-40mins
    5) Time period is generalized, make sure to monitor pot for boiling over!
    6) After time has passed, let the pot simmer and cool off (ENJOY HOMIE)

Additions to the hot pot

  • Noodles
  • Rice
  • Lettuce

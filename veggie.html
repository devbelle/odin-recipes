Odin Recipe: Veggies Base

This one is a cheat because this doesn't need to be cooked. Grocery stores have plenty of lettuce mixes to use or feel free to cook veggie base for your meal. In my opinion, a cooler mix base would be better for the hot pot but collard greens work wonders for it!

What ingredients are needed?

  • Lettuce mix or base of your choice
  • Spinach (raw or in can)

Cooking steps

    1) Boil or keep it raw lol
<0l>2) When boiling spinach or cabbage, i like to mix in cajun spices that work with the hot pot!
